description: >-
  Alaska's vast, untouched wilderness is best experienced from the water, where
  luxury cruise lines provide front-row access to calving glaciers, breaching
  whales, and hidden fjords without sacrificing five-star comfort.
insiderTip: >-
  Many travelers default to convenient round-trip sailings out of Seattle, but
  that often means spending too much of your itinerary cruising in open waters.
  For the ultimate Alaskan experience, I always advise booking a one-way cruise
  departing from either Vancouver or Anchorage (specifically out of the Whittier
  or Seward ports). This strategic routing allows for significantly more time
  exploring the actual port towns and gets you much deeper into the breathtaking
  glacier bays.
perk: >-
  Complimentary suite upgrade on select Silversea or Seabourn sailings and a
  private seaplane excursion to a remote glacial lodge.
